About STEM Careers Programme

A joint venture of HEC and PIEAS

STEM Careers Program is a joint venture of Higher Education Commission (HEC) and Pakistan Institute of Engineering & Applied Sciences (PIEAS) to inspire the potential youth of the nation to pursue careers in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM).

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about STEM programmes

What is STEM Careers Programme?

+

STEM Careers Programme is a joint venture of Higher Education Commission (HEC) and Pakistan Institute of Engineering & Applied Sciences (PIEAS) to inspire youth to pursue careers in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.

Who can apply for NSTC?

+

Students with aggregate marks of 60% or more in core subjects (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics) in their last exam and age less than 20 years at the time of participation in International Science Olympiads.

When are the screening tests conducted?

+

Nationwide screening tests are conducted annually in major cities across Pakistan. Check our announcements section for specific dates and locations.

What happens during training camps?

+

Top 50 students in each subject are invited for one week fully residential training camps. Through a series of evaluations and training sessions, this number is gradually reduced to 4-6 best students per subject for international representation.

How long has Pakistan been participating in International Olympiads?

+

Pakistan has been participating in International Physics Olympiad since 2001, Mathematics Olympiad since 2005, and Biology & Chemistry Olympiads since 2006. Our performance has been consistently encouraging with numerous medals.
